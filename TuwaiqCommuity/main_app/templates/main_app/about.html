A Better    Community

The Tuwaiq Community project aims to connect trainees from different BootCamps and build networks within the community.

The project will create a platform where trainees can communicate and learn from each other and access related groups based on their BootCamp.

The project will help trainees build relationships, gain valuable insights from other trainees in the community and develop skills that are essential for their career development, and will enhance the reputation of the Tuwaiq Community as a supportive and collaborative environment for trainees.
